ABOUT

B.U.G. Mafia (also known as "Bucharest Underground Mafia") is a Romanian hip hop group that was formed in 1993. The group consists of three members: Tataee (Vlad Irimia), Caddy (Cătălin Istrate), and Uzzi (Ionuț Vlăduț). They are considered one of the most influential and successful hip hop acts in Romania.

B.U.G. Mafia gained popularity in the mid-1990s with their debut album, "Mafia", which was one of the first hip hop albums released in Romania. Their music often addresses social and political issues, and they are known for their raw and aggressive style. They have released several successful albums, including "Născut și crescut în Pantelimon" (Born and Raised in Pantelimon), "Băieții Buni" (The Good Guys), and "Viata Noastra" (Our Life).

Over the years, B.U.G. Mafia has achieved significant commercial success in Romania and has had a major impact on the development of Romanian hip hop. They have won numerous awards and accolades for their music, and their songs continue to be popular in the Romanian music scene. B.U.G. Mafia is often recognized as one of the pioneers of Romanian hip hop and remains influential to this day.
